Our intensive, one-day interior design course is suitable for anyone, whether you want to reinforce a basic knowledge of interior design, or to add a professional finish to a specific home design project. The course also provides a good base on which to build for anyone interested in an interior design career.

Margot will take you step-by-step through the key principles of interior design. She will give you a basic knowledge of interior design and equip you with the insights and practical skills you need to produce beautiful and cost-effective schemes with confidence. You will learn how to use colour, how to design lighting schemes and how to design a room of your own. Margot will also look at some of the common and costly mistakes that amateur designers often make — and show you how to avoid them.

The course will cover:

  • An introduction to the design process
  • Colour theory and how to use colour with confidence
  • How to use pattern and texture to balance a design scheme
  • How to create the right mood and style for you
  • Lighting and creating an atmosphere
  • Space planning, ergonomics and scale drawing
  • Accessorising your scheme
  • Tricks of the design trade — how to get the most out of your designs
  • Common mistakes and how to avoid them

All students will be given a concise manual covering the key course topics to take away.

Please note: Places are strictly limited to 10 only.
